# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) principles.

- Be respectful and inclusive in all interactions.
- Provide constructive feedback; no harassment or personal attacks.
- Assume good intent, but address harmful behavior quickly.

To report violations, email the maintainers or open a confidential security issue (see `SECURITY.md`). Maintainers may take corrective action, including revoking access.
